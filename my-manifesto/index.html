Manifesto

To love what you do and feel that it matters — what could be more fun?

To me, happiness is a good coding challenge or puzzle, a glass of tea, and quiet time to find the answer.

What would you do if you knew you could not fail?

The world is moving so fast these days that the man who says it can't be done is generally interrupted by someone doing it.

Whatever you do, or dream you can, begin it. Boldness has genius, power, and magic in it.

LIFE

is not a dress rehearsal. It is also not a stress rehearsal. Relax, breathe, face the problem and fix it!

Humanity
is my race.
Love
is my religion.
Peace
is my weapon.

Listen.
Understand.
Learn. Then, and only then, can you start talking and be heard.

Facts do not disappear or change because you choose to ignore them.

It's all right to have butterflies in your stomach. Just get them to fly in formation.

You have to leave the city of your comfort and go into the wilderness of your intuition. What you'll discover will be wonderful. What you'll discover will be yourself.

Any idea becomes creative when the doer cares about doing it right, or better.

If you have built castles in the air, your work need not be lost; that is where they should be. Now put the foundations under them.
